Relationship

For Malagasy people Blood brotherhood also is the most important relationship. My Uncle has blood brotherhood Antandroy which tribe has own culture. His name is Limby. On 2017, Limby's father was dead so we went there to cheer up his family as we we called it Famangina_manjo because we took him as our family.

Feeling sad

Do you know what happened? I want to tell you that I was so shocked by their culture. I could see that they were not really sad about their father. All the night they sung and danced with a drum. I did not see even one of the family members crying. My Uncle knows their culture so he just followed what they asked him to do. I went home because I could stand seeing them dancing and singing but the corps was still there too. When I came home my mum asked me why did I go home. I explained to her; she was just laughing at me. The next day was the burial. They ran when they were carrying the dead body. I was feeling sick two days later. It was still in my mind what they did. Now, I know that it is better to have knowledge about different cultures.
